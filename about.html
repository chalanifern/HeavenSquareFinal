About Us

HeavenSquare is a Sri Lanka's best supermarket chain. It was established in the year 2000 in Colombo. Over the 22 years we have enhance by locating our branches across the country providing our customers with a world class retail experience. The dedicated employees are behind our success.Our main objective is to provide fresh and quality products to our customers. HeavenSquare buy fresh produces from our very own farms and local farmers. We ensure the produces reach the stores in 24 hours delivering on the promise of freshness.
